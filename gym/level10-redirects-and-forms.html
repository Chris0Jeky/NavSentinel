Level 10: Same-tab redirects and form submits

This level demonstrates same-tab navigation primitives.

  • Immediate redirect should usually be allowed (it is tightly coupled to the click).
  • Delayed redirect should be blocked and prompt (it fires after the gesture window).
  • Programmatic form submit should be blocked and prompt when triggered outside the gesture window.

Note: if you click Allow once the extension should execute the exact blocked navigation.
